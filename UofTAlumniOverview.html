U of T Alumni Overview

Welcome to the Career Learning Network (CLN)!

We recognize that career development does not stop once you graduate from university but continues to be an ongoing process. To support you in your career development process, we have provided you with a list of services that are offered by the University. We also acknowledge that in your role as an alumnus(?), you have a wonderful opportunity to support current students in their own career exploration process by mentoring or hosting students in your workplace. For additional information on how you can participate, please visit: https://cln.utoronto.ca/register/overview/hoststudents.htm

Career Services - Recent Alumni

Recent graduates continue to receive full access to all Career Centre services for up to 2 years following graduation.

Log in through Student & Recent Grads.

Your CLN account entitles you to:

  • View job postings for full-time job opportunities
  • Book one-on-one career counselling and employment advising services
  • Attend workshops, networking events and panels
  • Access online career resources

Alumni Beyond Two Years of Graduation

University of Toronto alumni beyond two years who are seeking career assistance from the University of Toronto can visit the U of T Alumni page for Hire, mentor, or share your expertise with a student or recent graduate. As a U of T alumnus, you might be looking for ways to become more involved in helping students in their own career development. Visit our employers and partners section and learn more about the following programs:

  • Extern Job Shadowing Program
  • Explore It Program
  • In the Field
  • Informational Interview database
  • The Next Steps Conference

Hire a Student or Graduate

Each year the Career Centre advertises over 40,000 positions and 12,000 volunteer opportunities. You can help a student or recent graduate develop the skills necessary to succeed. Hire them for part-time, full-time, summer, co-op or internship opportunities at your organization. For details on advertising a posting, please visit our job posting section.
