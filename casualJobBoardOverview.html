Casual Job Board Overview

If you are an individual looking to hire a student or recent graduate, our Casual Job Portal is the best place to find what you are looking for. The Casual Job Portal is reserved for job postings such as:

  • Child Care
  • Private Tutor
  • Home or lawn care assistance
  • Elderly or Special Needs Caregiver

The Casual Job Portal is reserved for individual users who are not hiring on behalf of an organization or company. To post a position for a specific organization, please visit our Employer Job Portal

If you do not currently have an account in our system, please register under our Casual Job User profile. You’ll be asked to provide some brief contact information prior to posting your job. Your registration and job posting will be approved within 2-3 business days.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact a member of the Career Centre Employer Recruitment & Engagement team directly at employers@mail.careers.utoronto.ca or (416) 978 8000
